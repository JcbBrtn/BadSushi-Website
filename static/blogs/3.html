Plot

Plot is the main component of a story. It dictates the flow of a story and can be broken down into 5 Key parts

  1. Introduction
  2. Rising Action
  3. Climax
  4. Falling Action
  5. Conslusion

These pieces strung in that order create most stories that we encounter. This form of story telling Allows for the reader to care about the situation and gives a satisfying conclusion to every story.

Character

What is a Character? A Person, animal or object in a literary work. Its more of a pshycological term than a physical term. These Characters are described through Characterization.

Character Motivation: The Why of how a character acts or thinks. There's three ways a character can act: Purpose, Impulse, Desire.
