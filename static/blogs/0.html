My Goals, and How I Achieve them

August 1, 2021

The Goals

My Goal The How
Intellectually Spend extra hours each day trying to push the levels of my capabilities. Continue to ask questions and pursue each answer. Be a student of life.
Creatively Keep the child-like spirit that keeps you going. This is where you keep asking why, and find the curiosity to keep looking.
Spritually Stay mindful. Stay present. And do not let your emotions dirve you forward. Control yourself, and use this clarity to find your cheif definite purpose. Pursue that purpose with everything you got.
Physically Compete. You have been around it enough in childhood. That was to prepare you for what is to come. Use it to stay driven when you lose that cheif aim.
Romantically Open yourself to the one you love. This world is rough, everyone needs someone. You have found yours, now use the love to help live a more meaningful life.

Goals are the backbone of our accomplishments. Its incredibly easy to be sucked into the drone of working everyday like you are working for your own goal; but end up working for another person's goals. You need to find what you want to do and do everything you can to acheive it. You have been given an incredible gift of life. It is time for you to do what you need to do to be successful. Be the incredible person you want to be. There is nothing stopping you from doing this. When you are afraid there are different things for you to think to calm yourself down.

  • Everyone you see and meet will be the same in the end... dead. embaressment will not last forever. In the end, the now is what is important.
  • You are doing what you can with what you have. The knowledge is also a piece of that.
  • The people that will make fun of you have lost hope in accomplishing things on their own. If they really saw what you are doing, they would respect it. Don't be afraid to see this in others as well.

It doesn't make sense to waist your talent because you think something is going to happen when you do what you want to do. You don't know what's going to happen because you've never done it before. And if you really want to do something, you wont let one bad experience stop you. Be the person you need to be. Ask for help when you need it. The only bad question is one that isn't asked. And someone who asks a question is a fool for a moment. Someone who doesn't ask questions is a fool for their lifetime.
